Getting to know us

Btresources is a company dedicated to providing quality ecommerce services to clients from both around and outside Africa. Our prime goal is to be a leading trusted ecommerce company in Africa. Some of the services we love to offer

  • Importation of goods into Namibia
  • Shipment of agricultural goods from around Africa.
  • Delivery of quality medical products and services

Btresources is a growing company at the moment serving as the ecommerce of three different companies. The companies are Flintwood Resources, Tk Titan Trading cc and Biotec Medical Suppies.
